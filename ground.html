My Friends

/*image*/

As you go through life, you pick up new friends. Some friends stay with you, others don't. I have been lucky enough to to gain or retain the friends that I need at whatever point I am in my path. I've learned that some inspire you, some provide you with comfort, others make you laugh, but they all help you enhance the truest version of yourself.

Mindfulness

Practicing mindfulness has helped me ground myself into a peaceful state of mind. It helps me think through difficult decisions and it helps me keep my mind clear.

There are different approaches to mindfulness. Currently, my favorite ways to practice mindfulness are through yoga, running, and meditation.
